Our StoryFunFlick Kids' Parties was born from a shared love of creativity, play, and family. A group of event planners, parents, and entertainers came together with one simple goal: to make every child’s birthday a day full of joy, laughter, and excitement. We saw a gap in the market for parties that didn’t just “tick the boxes” but truly captured the heart and imagination of both children and parents alike.
From humble beginnings, we’ve grown into a well-established team with a reputation for turning ordinary parties into extraordinary celebrations. What started as a small, passionate project has now become a trusted name among families looking for fun, personalized, and stress-free birthday experiences.
Our Mission & ValuesAt FunFlick, our mission is simple: We want every birthday to be a celebration of joy, creativity, and togetherness. We believe that birthdays are more than just a day on the calendar—they're an opportunity to create memories that will last a lifetime.
We operate on values that guide everything we do:
  • Imagination First: Every child is unique, and their party should reflect their personality. Whether it's superheroes or unicorns, we design events based on what makes each child’s heart sing.
  • Safety & Comfort: We prioritize the safety and well-being of every child. We work with trusted vendors, ensure age-appropriate activities, and maintain a friendly, professional environment.
  • Stress-Free for Parents: Planning a party doesn’t have to be overwhelming. We manage all the details—decor, games, food, and everything in between—so parents can relax and enjoy the party.
  • Environmental Responsibility: We care about our planet. We use eco-friendly materials where possible, from decorations to party favors, and encourage sustainable choices in every event.
Why Choose Us?There are plenty of party planners out there, but FunFlick stands out for a few key reasons:
  1. Custom-Built Experiences: Unlike generic party packages, we tailor everything to your child’s likes and interests. We don’t do "one-size-fits-all." From the invitations to the cake design, every detail is carefully curated to create a one-of-a-kind experience.
  2. Themed Parties That Wow: Our themes are built around creativity and imagination, so the birthday child feels like they’ve stepped into their dream world. Whether it’s a treasure hunt, an enchanted forest, or a space mission, we make the theme come to life in every corner of the event.
  3. Seamless Event Management: From booking the venue to packing up at the end, we handle everything. Parents can enjoy the party without worrying about any details. Our team arrives early to set up and stays to clean up, ensuring the event runs smoothly from start to finish.
  4. Entertainment That Engages: We don’t just throw parties; we create experiences. Our games, activities, and entertainers (who are all thoroughly vetted and experienced) are designed to keep kids engaged, active, and excited throughout the entire event.

What to Expect When You Work with Us
  • Consultation: Every party starts with a conversation. We’ll discuss your child’s interests, ideas for themes, and any special requests you have. This helps us create a vision for the party and give you a clear idea of what we can offer.
  • Personalized Planning: Once the theme is decided, we get to work. We handle everything from selecting vendors, organizing the entertainment, and choosing the right games and activities to setting up the space.
